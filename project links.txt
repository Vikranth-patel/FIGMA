project statement :
 Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC)---
The IRCTC and UTS applications suffer from poor usability, cluttered interfaces, confusing navigation, payment failures, and slow booking processes, creating frustration for millions of railway passengers. These issues become more critical during time-sensitive bookings like Tatkal and daily commuter travel, highlighting the need for a simpler, faster, and more user-friendly ticket booking experience.
